About
Background

Every new business starts with an idea; however, new businesses that do not test the feasibility of that idea are doomed to failure. Most small business owners cannot afford a full market analysis to test the feasibility of a product in a given market. The team here at SMART have made it possible for anyone to instantly spin up their own market research department.

Purpose

Our proprietary software allows users to gauge interest in a product or surface and geographically constrain those results to the area where the user intends to open their business. This allows the user to have a better understanding about whether the market is growing in that industry and whether customers are currently satisfied with the market as a whole.

How to get SMA[RT] Step 1: Provide the industry to which your potential business will belong. Step 2: Provide the region in which you are planning to open a business. Step 3: Click the 'execute' button. Step 4: Review your results and start profiting.
